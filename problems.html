The 1st Sustainable Development goal

Why is SDG 1 important?

It is important to make sure that all people live in normal conditions, being able to afford food, clean water, sanitary conditions and so on. But those aren't always free - which is why people need money.

The main problems are:

- Around 17% of the world's population still lives at or below $1,25 per day.
- People cannot afford food and medical care. Every human being should have access to those basic needs.
- Due to the lack of money and resources, people live in unsanitary conditions.
