The solutions for the problems

Right now, there are many important problems in the world. Here are some possible ways to diminish poverty.

- People do not have access to food and / or medical care.
To solve this problem:
- The UN or any other organisation should provide people in need medical help, resources such as food, building materials.
- People don't have stable jobs to provide them with money for a normal life.
- We should make it our goal to educate all the people in developing countries to help them find stable jobs and decrease the amount of unemployement.
